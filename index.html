about

In a picturesque corner of Tsarichanskiy district of Dnipropetrovsk region in the wilderness located recreation base “Wolshino”. River Orel bends around the base territory, the length of it is 320 km and the depth of up to 6 meters in some places. Orel is one of the cleanest rivers in Europe. Sandy bottom and clean water make it possible to enjoy the view of swimming fish and turtles.
